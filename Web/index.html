What can you expect to find here?

In Complainer Trainer you can find a host of learning topics about Complaints and Conflicts. From carefully selected articles and videos to exercises that will put into practice what you have learned, you can find it all within this website's pages!

Curated Content & Explanations

We start each of our topics with a short, easy to read explanations of the subject at hand. We then give you especially selected learning materials to supplement your understanding of the topic. From videos to articles, you can find it all here!

Digestible Presentation

We've seperated each distinct topic so as to not overwhelm you with information. Go through each subject seperately, at your own pace and without any pressure in our easy to use interface!

Exercises

There's no learning without practice. As such, each topic page contains a set of exercises at the end so that you can test out all that you've learned by going through our materials and solidify your learning experience.
